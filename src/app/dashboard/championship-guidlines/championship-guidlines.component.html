POINT SYSTEM

  • A college will get “50” points for every valid entry in the first fortnight
  • These points will decrease linearly every fortnight by “5” points. The point table is as follows:

    • Dates Points for every entry sent in the given time frame (Solo/Duet competitions) Points for every entry sent in the given time frame (Group competitions)
    25 September - 10 October 50 -
    11 October - 25 October 45 225
    26 October - 10 November 40 200
    11 November - 25 November 35 175
    25 November Onwards 30 150

  • The entry will be considered valid only if it follows every rule of the particular competition. Failure to do so may result in the college being disqualified from the overall championship.
  • There will be extra points for the winners of every competition.
