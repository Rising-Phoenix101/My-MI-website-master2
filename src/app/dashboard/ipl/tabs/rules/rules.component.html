Mood Indigo Premier League
Rules
  • The participating teams in auctions get disintegrated for the Premier League and all 3 members of a team play as individual players.
  • All members of the team are allotted the same players bought by them in auction.
  • The players bought are divided into 4 categories : Batsmen, Bowler, All Rounder and Wicketkeeper
  • In every match each participant has to nominate 3 players from his set of players. One of these 3 players would be MVP who will get double points
  • Nominations have to be made prior to toss i.e 30 mins before match time
  • The 3 players selected must include players from at least 2 categories
  • Then based on performance of players in the actual match, these 3 nominated players will be given points on certain set criterias as follows:
    Batting
    • + 1 point for every run scored
    • + 8 points for half century
    • +16 points for century (only century points then and not of half century as well i.e. not 24 points)
    • -10 points for duck out
    Bowling
    • + 25 for every wicket
    • + 8 for 4 wicket haul
    • + 16 for 5 wicket haul
    Fielding
    • + 8 for taking a catch
    • + 12 points for stumping
    • + 12 points for a direct runout
    • + 6 points to thrower involved in runout
    • +6 points to catcher involved in runout
  • Based on this scoring system points will be calculated for each participant and standings would be updated after every match
  • No points are awarded for any action in super over
  • A substitute player can only earn points via fielding
Note: In case of any discrepancy/misunderstanding during the entire league, the decision of the organizers would be final.
