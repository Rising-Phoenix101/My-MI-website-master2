Who is an Influencer?
Who is an Influencer?
An influencer is an individual who has garnered a certain following in a specific niche they actively engage with. The reason behind their influence is the fact that their work and opinions for that particular niche are valued by and prove to have an impact on their followers. Influencers are viewed as friends whose audiences feel a connect with, want to emulate and buy into.

FAQs
Who can register?
Any college student with a public Instagram account and 1000+ Instagram followers can register.
Is the program for other social media platforms as well?
Yes, the Influencer program is for all the major social media platforms including Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter and Snapchat. You can register all of your social media accounts, ALTHOUGH an active Instagram public account with 1000+ followers is the qualification criteria.
How is this program different from the Indigo Squad/College Connect Program?
The Influencer program is focused on developing your social media and content creation skills. For a more holistic program focused on building essential soft skills register for the College Connect Program at my.moodi.org/ccp.
Can I be a part of both CCP and the Influencer Program?
Yes, you can register for both the programs. *To register for Influencer program you must have a qualifying Instagram account
How do referrals work?
TYou will be given a referral code. You can recommend your friends to register for the Influencer Program. Whenever your friend registers using your referral code, you will earn points.
What kind of social media tasks will be given?
A topic/base would be given to you. Based on that you will be creating your own post or video, writing your tweet, making a shoutout video, sharing a story etc. The types and details of the posts will be given to you. A base creative from the Mood Indigo team will be provided as well to help beginners who can’t create their own content.
