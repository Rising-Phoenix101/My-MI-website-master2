How does MI Coin work?

The MI coins can be used to purchase things such as merchandise, accommodation, etc and many more exciting products such as headphones and bluetooth speakers.

How do I earn MI Coins?

The coins can be earned by registering in various programs like CCP, IAP and Competitions. There will be regular tasks available in CCP through which you can earn a lot of MI Coins.

Are MI Coins transferable to other participants?

Sorry! You cannot transfer MI Coins to another participant. You can use it at MI Shop for all transactions.

Do MI Coins expire?

Yes. If you do not show any activity on CCP portal for straight one month, then all your MI Coins will expire.
